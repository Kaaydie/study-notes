A flashlight uses an LR6/AA (1.5V) battery and a 3 Ω bulb (which behaves as a resistor, at its nominal operating point). Find the efficiency of the power transfer, in %, assuming the battery has an internal resistance of 0.1Ω
![[git-sync/Sem1/IEEC sem1/Pasted image 20251104131346.png]]
$$
\begin{align}
 & I=\frac{E_{0}}{R_{t}+R_{B}}=\frac{1.5V}{3.1Ω} \\
 & Ŋ=\frac{I_{2}R_{B}}{I*E_{0}}≈97\% 
\end{align}
$$
